Mechanical Design - ENGR 3330

Mechanical Design teaches computer aided design and technical drawing skills. The course this semester was centered on creating wind sculptures for the Olin "O". We learned structures, cams, linkages, and transmissions to create our wind sculptures in pairs and groups.
